1.2 Google Account and Avatar, YouTube Channel Name, YouTube Channel ID

Collection: When you use livestreaming to YouTube feature of Recoder Screen, you need to use a Google Account to log in and authorize on Recoder Screen. When you log in, we will get your Google account and avatar, YouTube channel name and YouTube channel ID. If you do not use this feature, we will not collect the information.

Aim: We use the information to help you livestream your screen to the YouTube channel of your Google account.

1.3 Subscriptions and Subscriber Lists on YouTube

Collection: Recoder Screen will get the subscription number of your YouTube channel when you livestream while the Recoder Screen - livestreaming tools – live subscription goal feature is enabled; Recoder Screen will get the subscription number and the subscriber list of your YouTube channel when you livestream while the Recoder Screen - livestreaming tools - subscription alerts feature is enabled. If you do not use the features, we will not collect the information.

Aim: Show the current subscription number of your YouTube channel and your subscription goals to you and your audience; Pop up an alert like User name just subscribed to your channel on screen and an audio alert when your YouTube channel gets a new subscription.

1.4 Facebook Account and Avatar

Collection: When you use livestreaming to Facebook feature of Recoder Screen, you need to use a Facebook account to log in and authorize on Recoder Screen. When you log in, we will get your Facebook account and avatar. If you do not use this feature, we will not collect the information.

Aim: We use the information to help you livestream your screen to your Facebook account.

1.5 The Facebook Pages in which Your User Identity is Admin, Editor or Live Contributor

Collection: When you use the livestreaming to a Facebook page feature of Recoder Screen, we will get the Facebook pages in which your user identity is admin, editor or live contributor. If you do not use this feature, we will not collect the information.

Aim: Show the list of Facebook pages for you to choose. After you select a Facebook page, your screen contents can be streamed to the selected page.

1.6 Twitch Account and Avatar, Twitch Channel Name and Channel Address

Collection: When you use livestreaming to Twitch feature of Recoder Screen, you need to use Twitch account to log in and authorize in Recoder Screen. When you log in, we will get your Twitch account and avatar, Twitch channel name and channel address. If you do not use this feature, we will not collect the information.

Aim: We use the information to help you livestream your screen to your Twitch channel.

1.7 Screen On and Off

Collection: When the screen of your device turns on or turns off, we will get the status(on/off) of your screen.

Aim: Recoder Screen provides the option “Not stop recording when screen is off”. Therefore, it is necessary to acquire the status of screen in order to make the feature work.

1.8 Application Package Name

Collection: When you record screen or capture screenshots with Recoder Screen, we will get the application package names of the apps that you capture; when you use DU Recorder to share the videos to the third-party platforms, we will get the application package names of the third-party apps that you select. If you do not use these features, we will not collect the information.

Aim: With the information, Recoder Screen optimizes the apps that users record for most times to provide a better recording and live experience. Recoder Screen will provide you with a personalized display order of the third-party platforms based on your sharing history, which makes your sharing more convenient.
